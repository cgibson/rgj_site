HELLO, WORLD


What is the remote game jam?

The Remote Game Jam is an accelerated game development event. Like other jams (Global Game Jam and Ludum Dare) participants have 48 hours to create a game.

What sets this event apart from the others is that our jam locations are spread across the world, and teams must have participants in different jam locations.

How to play

You don't need to have participated in a jam before. In fact, you don't even need to know how to code, though we recommend you check our resources section if you want to do some preparation.

Here are some ways you can participate:
Game Artwork
Writing & Story
